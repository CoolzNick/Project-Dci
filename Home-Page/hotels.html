LA PERLA VILLAS & SUITES

a Perla Villas & Suites is a luxurious boutique hotel located in the heart of the prestigious village of Oia, Santorini. Its complex of elegant cave houses and villas is built on the famous caldera of Oia and offers stunning unobstructed views of the volcano and the Aegean Sea, coupled with top-of-the-notch service. Officially characterized as "traditionally built" by the Greek National Tourism Organization (GNTO), La Perla Villas & Suites harmonically blends old and new architectural elements, in a modern decor with luxurious amenities.
